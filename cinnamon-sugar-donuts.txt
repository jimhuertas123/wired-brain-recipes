Homemade Cinnamon Sugar Donuts

INGREDIENTS

For the Donuts:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup granulated sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- ½ tsp salt
- ⅓ cup milk
- ¼ cup melted butter
- 1 large egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the Cinnamon Sugar Coating:
- ½ cup granulated sugar
- 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
- 3 tbsp melted butter

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a donut pan with butter or cooking spray.

2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.

3. In another bowl, combine milk, melted butter, egg, and vanilla.

4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Don't overmix.

5. Spoon batter into donut pan, filling each cavity about ¾ full.

6. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until donuts spring back when lightly touched.

7. Cool in pan for 5 minutes before turning out onto a wire rack.

8. For coating, mix sugar and cinnamon in a shallow bowl.

9. Brush warm donuts with melted butter, then roll in cinnamon sugar mixture.

10. Serve immediately for best texture.

PREP TIME: 15 minutes
COOK TIME: 15 minutes
SERVINGS: 12 donuts

TIPS
- Don't overmix the batter to keep donuts tender
- Best served fresh and warm
- Can be stored in airtight container for 2 days
- If you don't have a donut pan, you can make muffins instead
