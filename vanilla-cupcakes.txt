Classic Vanilla Cupcakes

INGREDIENTS

For the Cupcakes:
- 1½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- ⅓ cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1½ tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- ⅔ cup milk
- 1 large egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the Vanilla Buttercream:
- ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
- 3 cups powdered sugar
- 3-4 tbsp milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

INSTRUCTIONS

For the Cupcakes:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.

2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

3. Beat in egg and vanilla extract.

4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.

5. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk to the butter mixture, beginning and ending with flour mixture.

6. Divide batter evenly among cupcake liners, filling each about ⅔ full.

7. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

8. Cool in pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.

For the Buttercream:
1. Beat butter until light and fluffy.

2. Gradually add powdered sugar, beating until smooth.

3. Add milk, vanilla, and salt. Beat until creamy and spreadable.

4. Frost cooled cupcakes using a piping bag or offset spatula.

PREP TIME: 20 minutes
COOK TIME: 20 minutes
SERVINGS: 12 cupcakes

TIPS
- Room temperature ingredients mix more easily
- Don't overmix batter to avoid tough cupcakes
- Store frosted cupcakes in refrigerator for up to 3 days
