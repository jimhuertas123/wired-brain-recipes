Rich Chocolate Brownies

INGREDIENTS

- ½ cup unsalted butter
- 4 oz dark chocolate, chopped
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ cup chocolate chips (optional)
- ½ cup chopped walnuts (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper.

2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt butter and dark chocolate together in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval until smooth.

3. Stir in sugar until well combined.

4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.

5. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, and salt.

6. Fold the dry ingredients into the chocolate mixture until just combined.

7. Stir in chocolate chips and walnuts if using.

8. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread evenly.

9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

10. Cool completely in pan before cutting into squares.

PREP TIME: 15 minutes
COOK TIME: 30 minutes
SERVINGS: 16 brownies

TIPS
- Don't overbake - brownies should be slightly underdone for fudgy texture
- Let cool completely before cutting for clean edges
- Store covered at room temperature for up to 4 days
