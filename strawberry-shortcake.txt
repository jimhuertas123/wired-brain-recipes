Fresh Strawberry Shortcake

INGREDIENTS

For the Shortcakes:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup granulated sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- 6 tbsp cold unsalted butter, cubed
- ¾ cup heavy cream
- 1 large egg

For the Strawberry Topping:
- 2 lbs fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
- ¼ cup granulated sugar
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

For Assembly:
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 3 tbsp powdered sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS

For the Shortcakes:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

3. Cut in cold butter using a pastry cutter or two knives until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

4. In a small bowl, whisk together cream and egg.

5. Pour cream mixture into flour mixture and stir just until dough comes together.

6. Turn dough onto floured surface and gently pat into a ¾-inch thick rectangle.

7. Cut into 8 squares or use a round cutter for circles.

8. Place on prepared baking sheet and bake for 15-18 minutes until golden brown.

For the Strawberries:
1. Combine sliced strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice in a bowl.

2. Let sit for 30 minutes to allow juices to develop.

For Assembly:
1. Whip cream with powdered sugar and vanilla until soft peaks form.

2. Split shortcakes in half horizontally.

3. Place bottom half on plate, top with strawberries and whipped cream.

4. Add top half of shortcake and more strawberries and cream.

PREP TIME: 25 minutes
COOK TIME: 18 minutes
SERVINGS: 8 shortcakes

TIPS
- Keep butter cold for flakier shortcakes
- Don't overwork the dough
- Serve immediately after assembly
- Fresh berries work best, but frozen can be substituted
